Interview with a Vampire

How did you get started into coding?

One day working in my store on the weekend. Stanley, a regular lotto customer, witnessed me and my son Ashton studying spelling words for his upcoming Scripps regional spelling bee competition. He reminisced how he and his son had also done the same routine. He had recommended to me if your son can pick up things quick to have him try his luck at coding. How everything these days is going into "the cloud". I followed his suggestion, and not only did I think it was good for him, but heck why not me as well.....Hence the phrase "I code therefore I am!"
